CloseCut

Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 10, 2026

CloseCut is a private movie and series journal app created by Azul Grisel Ramírez Kuri.

This Privacy Policy explains what information CloseCut collects, how it is used, and how it is protected.

Information We Collect

Account Information

If you create an account, CloseCut may collect information such as your email address, display name, and authentication identifier.

User Content

CloseCut lets you create and save personal movie and series journal entries, ratings, notes, watch history, plans, circles, and related preferences.

App Data and Preferences

CloseCut may store app preferences such as language, appearance settings, saved titles, and onboarding choices.

Firebase and App Services

CloseCut uses Firebase services for authentication, database storage, app security, and reliability. These services may process identifiers, authentication data, diagnostic data, and app activity needed to operate the app.

Crash and Diagnostic Data

If crash reporting or diagnostic tools are enabled, CloseCut may receive technical information about crashes, errors, device type, operating system version, and app performance.

How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • provide and operate CloseCut;
  • let you sign in and access your private journal;
  • save and sync your movie and series data;
  • support private circles, plans, and shared features;
  • improve app reliability and security;
  • diagnose crashes and technical issues;
  • protect the app from abuse or unauthorized access.

Sharing of Information

CloseCut does not sell your personal information.

Your private journal content is not public by default. Some features may allow you to share selected content with circles or trusted people inside the app. You control what you choose to share.

We may use trusted service providers, such as Firebase, to operate core app functionality.

Data Storage

CloseCut may store data locally on your device and, when account or sync features are used, in cloud services such as Firebase.

Data Deletion

You may delete content inside the app where supported. CloseCut also includes account-related settings such as sign out and delete account flows.

For data deletion requests or privacy questions, contact: blueintechofficial@gmail.com

Children’s Privacy

CloseCut is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as CloseCut evolves. If changes are made, the effective date will be updated.
