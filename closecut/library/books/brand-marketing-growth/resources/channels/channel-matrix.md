# Channel Matrix

| Channel | Primary role | Primary audience | Primary CTA | Do not optimize for |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Website | Explain, qualify, and convert | All prospective users | See how it works / Join beta | Raw traffic without comprehension |
| App Store | Validate expectation and install | Ready-to-install iOS users | Download | Keyword reach detached from fit |
| TestFlight | Structured learning | Qualified beta testers | Test a focused flow | Tester count alone |
| Instagram | Visual product and memory storytelling | Reflective viewers and film community | Visit landing page | Trend participation alone |
| X | Product decisions and indie community | Developers, designers, early adopters | Read / test / discuss | Repetitive promotion |
| LinkedIn | Case studies and professional credibility | Product, engineering, press, partners | Read the product story | Generic founder motivation |
| Press | Third-party understanding | Reviewers and media audiences | Learn / review | Coverage volume without accuracy |
