# Campaign Lifecycle

1. Define the product decision and current evidence.
2. Select one audience, one job, one message, and one CTA.
3. Build the creative system from current product screens.
4. Run claim, privacy, accessibility, and release-state review.
5. Adapt assets by channel role rather than duplicating them.
6. Publish with named monitoring and correction ownership.
7. Measure the predeclared signal and gather qualitative evidence.
8. Record the decision, learning, and asset deprecation status.
